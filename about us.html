About Us

Welcome to

Exotica Cafe and Restro

Welcome to Swarg Exotica and Restro Samastipur’s Most Loved Restaurant A Passion for Food, Served with Love Looking for a place that feels like home, serves food that touches the soul, and offers an atmosphere perfect for every mood? Welcome to Swarg Exotica, Samastipur’s favorite spot for fine dining, family meals, celebrations, and more.

Location

Located at Magardahi Ghat, Swarg Exotica and Restrois more than just a restaurant — it’s a place where flavors come alive, where every meal is made with care, and every guest is treated like family. Whether you're here for a quick lunch, a romantic dinner, or a big celebration, we promise you one thing: great taste, great service, and great memories.

Dine. Celebrate. Repeat. – Only at Swarg Exotica.

At Swarg Exotica, our journey began with a simple goal — to bring high-quality, delicious food to the people of Samastipur in a warm and welcoming environment. Since then, we’ve grown into one of the city’s most trusted restaurants, known for our diverse menu, beautiful ambiance, and consistent service.

Specialities

We believe that food isn’t just about taste — it’s about experience. That’s why every dish at Swarg Exotica and Restro is carefully prepared using fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and a touch of creativity. From spicy North Indian curries and classic Chinese dishes to comforting tandoori platters and local favorites — we’ve got something for everyone.

One for all

Whether it’s a family dinner, a birthday party, a casual outing with friends, or a corporate meal — Swarg Exotica and Restro is the place where food and memories come together. Because good food brings people closer — and that’s what we’re here for. Dine. Celebrate. Repeat. – Only at Swarg Exotica. ---
